HOW IT WORKS

Quiller is an advanced assassination game free of violence and malice. Every user recieves a unique QR code which must be printed out and placed onto any visible part of his or her body. This QRcode will be targeted by other uers in proximity.

Each user is also assigned an enemy, the QR code of whom he must scan. With every scan, the user gains one kill and is re-assigned a target. The game requires stealth and vigilance. Play at your own risk.
